Our Story

Bridging traditional wisdom with modern analytics.

Elbaseety Institute was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic knowledge and modern analytical expertise.

With a strong foundation in statistics, data science, and Islamic inheritance law, the institute serves as a trusted platform for individuals and organizations seeking clarity, accuracy, and excellence.

What began as a personal mission to provide structured and reliable solutions has grown into a developing institution committed to impact, education, and service.

From Jos to a broader audience, the goal remains the same: to deliver knowledge with precision and integrity.

Our Core Values

  • Precision: Accuracy in every calculation.
  • Integrity: Trust in Shariah and Data.
  • Excellence: Global standards in service.
